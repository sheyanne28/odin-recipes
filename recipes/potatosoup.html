Potato Soup

Description:

If you are on of those pesky vegetarians, like myself, you may find yourself longing for a soup that is friendly to you. Well, long no further for you have found the jackpot! You don't even have to miss out on all the creamy, flavorful goodness with this vegetarian option-vegans you have to keep searching though.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (surprising I know)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Stock
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cut your veggies and potatoes down to a bite size.
  2. Saute the veggies.
  3. Add broth and potatoes. Cook until potatoes are softened.
  4. Smash potatoes to make you soup extra creamy.
  5. Next add your cheese, sour cream, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Lastly, gulp down that pipping hot soup you earned!
